About

Rules: Swipe left, right, up or down to move the pieces on the board. One boy and one girl piece can be combined to make a heart. Hearts of equal values can be combined to make hearts of greater values. Make as much love as possible!

Bonus points: You can earn bonus points by upgrading hearts in an ascending sequence. Ie: 2,4, 8....

Bonus multiplier: One square on the board is highlighted. If you upgrade create a heart in that square any bonus points earned from that sequene will multiplied.

Controls: Mouse - swipe gestures. Keyboard - arrows or aswd.
